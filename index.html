RoofNow · New Standard Restoration

Address in,
measurement report out.

RoofNow turns a property address into a contractor-grade roof & wall report — per-facet area, pitch, azimuth, roofing squares, ridge/hip/valley lengths, plus wall area with window & door openings subtracted.

Target accuracy: < 3% area error vs. ground truth
Roof

Per-facet geometry

Total area, roofing squares, and each facet's pitch, azimuth, and ridge/hip/valley/eave/rake lengths with a suggested waste factor.

Walls

Derived elevations

Gross wall area with an N/S/E/W breakdown, gable triangles, and window/door openings subtracted for net siding area.

Data paths

Blended & resilient

Google Solar API for instant geometry, USGS 3DEP LiDAR plane-fitting for coverage gaps, and drone photogrammetry for high-value jobs.
