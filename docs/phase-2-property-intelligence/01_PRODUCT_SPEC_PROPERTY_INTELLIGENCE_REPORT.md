# Product Spec — Property Intelligence Report

## Purpose
Turn a price range into a believable homeowner-facing report.

## Required Sections
1. Property header
2. Roof snapshot
3. AI summary
4. Measurement confidence explanation
5. Good / Better / Best pricing
6. Why this estimate?
7. Roof health placeholder
8. Storm exposure placeholder
9. Recommended next step
10. Free verification inspection CTA

Do not build CRM features.
